Our Services

Colonoscopy

A procedure to examine the colon for polyps, cancer, and other abnormalities.

Upper GI Endoscopy

An examination of the upper part of the gastrointestinal tract to diagnose and treat conditions affecting the esophagus, stomach, and duodenum.

Gastroenteritis Treatment

Care and management for inflammation of the gastrointestinal tract, typically caused by infections.

Capsule Endoscopy

A diagnostic procedure using a small, pill-sized camera to capture images of the small intestine.

ERCP

A technique combining endoscopy and fluoroscopy to diagnose and treat problems of the bile and pancreatic ducts.
